--- category: projects title: Warzone ---

Warzone is an android game in which the main charcter is a stickman stuck in a war field. Your goal is to help him dodge all the missiles falling from above.
I have developed this game with the help of my cousin Saumil Shah. The coding is done by me, and graphics for the game are made by him.

You can download the game here

The code is on github. Feel free to contibute to it :). Issues and bugs can be posted there or feel free to message me on facebook, or email me :). Contact details are on the home page


The controls are pretty simple

  • Tilt phone left/right to move the stickman
  • Swipe up to jump
  • Swipe down to slide
